# Cybersecurity Learning Journey 🛡️

Welcome to my personal knowledge base. This repository serves as a central hub for my technical notes, lab reports, and research as I navigate the cybersecurity landscape.

## 🎯 Primary Focus
My main objective is mastering the intersection of **Defensive Operations (Blue Team)** and **Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)**. I believe that effective security is built on a foundation of strong policy and proactive defense.

---

## 🏗️ Knowledge Pillars

### 📋 GRC (Governance, Risk, & Compliance)
*Focus: Aligning security with business goals and regulatory requirements.*
- **Frameworks:** Studying NIST CSF, ISO/IEC 27001, and SOC2.
- **Risk Management:** Risk assessment methodologies and mitigation strategies.
- **Policy & Documentation:** Developing security policies and incident response plans.

### 🛡️ Blue Team (Defensive Security)
*Focus: Detecting, responding to, and neutralizing threats.*
- **Security Operations:** SIEM monitoring (Splunk/ELK), log analysis, and alerting.
- **Incident Response:** Threat hunting and digital forensics.
- **Hardening:** Network security, IAM, and endpoint protection.

### ⚔️ Red Team (Offensive Experimentation)
*Focus: Understanding the adversary to better the defense.*
- **Purpose:** I use offensive tactics strictly for **knowledge and experimentation** to validate defensive controls.
- **Lab Work:** Penetration testing basics, vulnerability scanning, and exploit research.
- **Purple Teaming:** Mapping technical exploits to GRC risk registers.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Defensive:** Splunk, Wireshark, Microsoft Defender for Cloud.
- **Offensive:** Kali Linux, Nmap, Burp Suite (Experimental).
- **Compliance:** NIST Self-Assessment tools, GRC platforms.
